Join Us

Welcome to ISOC (Islington Octopush Club)

We are always happy to see new people of any skill level, age or sex: if you can swim we can teach you to snorkel, if you can snorkel we can teach you to play Underwater Hockey and enjoy it.

We normally have 2 - 3 novice players undergoing training and 15 to 20 players ranging from beinners to internationals and several exinternational players!

New Players

Join us and try out your first 4 sessions for free

  • If you can swim, we can teach you to snorkel!
  • If you can snorkel, we can quickly teach you to play Octopush and like it

We can supply all the equipment you need (mark, fins, gloves, hat and snorkel) for those who just want to have a go or have yet to made up their minds.

You do HAVE to bring your own swimsuit and towel.

Existing Players

  • If you have your kit, you can just let us know when you'll turn up.
  • If you need kit, let us know, the club has some loan kit.

Contact Nia (niaphi at hotmail.com) to get involved

When and Where

Our training sessions are Thursday nights 20:30-21.30 at St George's Pool, Shadwell. (221 The Highway, Tower Hamlets, E1 9BP. Tel. 020 7709 9714)

The closest station Shadwell East London tube & DLR (the roads from the station to the pool are fairly busy)

There is parking round the back of pool (off The Highway)

Be at Pool Side 20:25 ready to play!

Fees

New players Come along and try it out for free for the first four sessions!!

We have a supply of club loan kit, no charge

There are two fees; Club membership and British Octopush Association.

Club membership

  • Unlimited Swimming: £130 every half year
  • Pay as you Go: £70 for 10 sessions, £37.5 for 5 sessions or £9 per session

British Octopush Association

  • Annual fee of £30

About Us

Islington Octopush (Underwater Hockey) Club started out over 40 years ago as a BSAC (British Sub-Aqua Club) Octopush team. We are now independent members of the British Octopush Association (BOA), but still have links with several diving clubs and many of our players continue to dive.
